The Theater Club helps ASOIU students to get together and reveal their talent and creative potential. The purpose of the Club is to jointly learn the intricacies of acting, giving special training to students interested in theatre.

The Theater Club presents performances and scenes in the spirit of the events held at the University. In addition, retrospective programs by genres, panel discussions and negotiations are organised with amateur script teachers and students with acting skills.
